Bodhi

Event pre-booking website for National level technical fest.

Bodhi 2024 & 2025, website developed for pre-booking events of Bodhi, the national level tech-fest held at Viswajyothi College of Engineering and Technology during both the years 2024 and 2025.

WHAT THEY NEED

The object that they have given to me was to develop a website where people can explore and pre-book any interested events. Website should have:
• Home page: Starting with a small introduction of Bodhi then showcasing all the main events with a small description.
• Event page: Lists out all the events categorized with the department which conducts each event.
• Contact page: Where all coordinators contact will be showcased in case of any enquiries.

This is what I’ve decrypted from what they told me.

THAT HELPING HAND!

Akshay Shine Krishna, the one who helped me to collect and upload all the necessary datas. Which was a great help at this very tight schedule!

WHAT I DO

The deadline that they’ve given to me to complete this work was very small...like 5 days!
That will be a very tight schedule to develop this by code. So i planned to develop this any no-code web development tool.
I’ve already used wordpress with elementor(plugin). I swear that thing is not designed for the biginers...so i choose wix studio, ahh...slightly better!
